# 🤖 Dumb Ways to AI

> *"Build AI. Don't break society."*

An educational browser-based game teaching 8th graders about the principles 
of Responsible AI through fast-paced, WarioWare-style microgames.

🎮 **Play here:** https://victoriamjohnson.github.io/dumb-ways-to-ai/

---

## 📖 About

**Dumb Ways to AI** is a K-12 educational game developed as part of a 
research project at the University of Texas at San Antonio (UTSA). 

Players take on the role of a junior developer at **ResponsibleCity AI Labs**, 
where the villainous **Developer Doom** keeps shipping reckless AI systems that 
harm the city. Your mission: counter his bad designs, earn the title of 
**Certified Responsible Developer**, and climb the leaderboard.

The game is designed around the **AI4K12 Big Idea 5: Societal Impact** 
framework and targets 8th grade learners (ages 13–14).

---

## 🎯 Learning Objectives

Players will learn to identify and apply the **4 Principles of Responsible AI**:

| Principle | What it teaches |
|---|---|
| 🏀 **Be Fair** | AI trained on unbalanced data produces unfair decisions |
| 📺 **Be Transparent** | Users should always know when AI is involved |
| 🔒 **Protect Privacy** | Developers should only collect data the AI actually needs |
| ✅ **Take Accountability** | Developers are responsible for their AI even after deployment |

---

## 🕹️ How to Play

1. Enter your name and grade level to create your profile
2. Play through the **Tutorial** to learn each principle with Dr. Bot as your guide
3. Take on **Challenge Mode** — a 2-minute timed gauntlet where all 4 microgames 
   play in random order at increasing difficulty
4. Earn **Responsible Badges** (points) for correct decisions
5. Climb the **Responsible Developer Leaderboard**
6. Sign the **Responsible AI Pledge** at the end

## 🎮 The Microgames

### 🏀 Balance the Dataset — *Fairness*
Developer Doom trained a co-ed basketball recruiting AI using mostly boy 
players. Balance the dataset before the season starts.

### 📺 Label the AI Feature — *Transparency*
Doom's platform DevDoomTube posts AI-generated comments using real users' 
names with no labels. Stamp the "AI Feature" label before it slides past.

### 📋 Override the Mistake — *Accountability*
Doom's AI grading system is failing students and he walked away from it. 
Mash SPACE to push the correction patch before report cards go out.

### 🔒 Protect User Privacy — *Privacy*
Doom is launching DoomGPT and wants to collect everything. Toggle ON only 
the data the AI actually needs — or press X to reject both options.

## 📊 Research

This game is part of an undergraduate research project studying whether 
game-based learning can improve middle school students' understanding of 
Responsible AI principles.

**Data collected (locally, no cloud):**
- Student name and grade level
- Score and badges remaining
- Microgames passed/failed with timestamps
- Time to completion

Pre and post surveys measure learning outcomes across the 4 principles.
